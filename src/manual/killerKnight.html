Killer Knight

Killer knight is played in 8x8 chessboard, each player has a knight and a set of special pieces called "diamonds", diamonds can't move. You can choose how many diamonds each player owns in options at Select Game dialog window. Options are 8, 12, or 16 diamonds. White knight starts at "a1" square and black knight starts at "h8" square.


Killer knight start position with 8 diamonds.

Knight has special movement that describes 'L' character and is only piece that can jump another pieces. Knight captures a piece if this piece stays in last square of its movement.


Knight movement.

To win the game, player must capture all opponent diamonds, player also wins if capturing opponent knight. After a player to capture all opponent diamonds, game ends, and in this case, no knight can capture opponent knight.

Win positions examples

  1. Win position for white: white knight captured all opponent diamonds. This position is end game and black knight can't capture white knight.
  2. Win position for black: black knight captured all opponent diamonds.
  3. Win position for black: black knight captured white knight. Note that both players have diamonds in chessboard.
