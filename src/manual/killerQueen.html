Killer Queen

Killer queen is played in 8x8 chessboard, each player has a queen and a set of special pieces called "diamonds", diamonds can't move. You can choose how many diamonds each player owns in options at Select Game dialog window. Options are 8, 12, or 16 diamonds. White queen starts at "a1" square and black queen starts at "h8" square.


Killer queen start position with 8 diamonds.

Queen can move any number of squares in rank, column, or diagonal, and captures in same way. Queen can't jump another pieces.


Queen movement.

To win the game, player must capture all opponent diamonds, player also wins if capturing opponent queen. After a player to capture all opponent diamonds, game ends, and in this case, no queen can capture opponent queen.

Win positions examples

  1. Win position for white: white queen captured all opponent diamonds.
  2. Win position for black: black queen captured all opponent diamonds. This position is end game and white queen can't capture black queen.
  3. Win position for white: white queen captured black queen. Note that both players have diamonds in chessboard.
