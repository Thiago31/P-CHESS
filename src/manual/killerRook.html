Killer Rook

Killer rook is played in 8x8 chessboard, each player has a rook and a set of special pieces called "diamonds", diamonds can't move. You can choose how many diamonds each player owns in options at Select Game dialog window. Options are 8, 12, or 16 diamonds. White rook starts at "a1" square and black rook starts at "h8" square.


Killer rook start position with 8 diamonds.

Rook can move any number of squares in rank, or column, and captures in same way. Rook can't jump another pieces.


Rook movement.

To win the game, player must capture all opponent diamonds, player also wins if capturing opponent rook. After a player to capture all opponent diamonds, game ends, and in this case, no rook can capture opponent rook.

Win positions examples

  1. Win position for white: white rook captured all opponent diamonds.
  2. Win position for black: black rook captured all opponent diamonds. This position is end game and white rook can't capture black rook.
  3. Win position for white: white rook captured black rook. Note that both players have diamonds in chessboard.
