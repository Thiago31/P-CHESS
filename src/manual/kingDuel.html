King Duel

King duel is played in 8x8 chessboard, each player owns only a king. White king starts in 1st rank and black king starts in 8th rank. Starting column is randomly chosen and both kings starts at same column. As example, if it is chosen column "c", white king starts at "c1" square and black king starts at "c8" square.


King Duel start position at column "c".

King can move only one square in rank, column or diagonal, two kings can't stay adjacent squares, it must have at least one square between them.


King movement.


King movement. In this position, white king can't move to "d5", "e5", or "f5", since black king attacks these squares.

White king must arrive 8th rank, and black king must avoid white king arriving 8th rank. You can configure max number of movements in options at Select Game dialog window, options are 10, 20, 30, or 40 max number of movements. If white king arrives 8th rank, white player wins, if white king doesn't arrives 8th rank after max number of movements, black player wins.
